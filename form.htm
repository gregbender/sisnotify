Frequently Asked Questions - or just some stuff I want to say

1)  What does this thing do?
  • Is there a course that you really need to get into, but it's closed?  Rather than spending your entire day sitting on SIS watching and waiting for it to open, this site will take care of it for you.  Enter your email address and the course number of the class you wish to get into.  This site will check SIS every 15 minutes to see if the course ever becomes open.  If it does become open, you will receive an email letting you know.
2)  Why did you make this?
  • Because I always hated as a freshman not being able to get into a course, and I remember many times spending a few hours just sitting in front of SIS hitting refresh. Some people may find that fun, I however am not one of them.
3)  If everyone starts using this, what's the point?

  • True.... Technically if everyone started to use this service, then your chances of getting into the course you want is about the same as spending endless hours hitting refresh. So your right...what is the point.

4)  Do I need to use my RIT email account?

  • No, any email address will work.
